GET a la ruta base
curl -v -XGET -H 'Content-type: application/json'  'http://localhost:8080/api/v1/economics-stats'

POST a la ruta base
curl -v -XPOST -H 'Content-type: application/json' -d '{ "province": "Granada", "year": 2006, "expensive_peu": 2544125, "expensive_id": 25336, "employers_id": 5412 }' 'http://localhost:8080/api/v1/economics-stats

GET a un recurso 
curl -v -XGET -H 'Content-type: application/json'  'http://localhost:8080/api/v1/economics-stats/Sevilla/2009'

DELETE a un recurso
curl -v -XDELETE -H 'Content-type: application/json'  'http://localhost:8080/api/v1/economics-stats/Sevilla/2009'

PUT a un recurso
curl -v -XPUT -H 'Content-type: application/json' -d '{ "province": "Sevilla", "year": 2009, "expensive_peu": 999999, "expensive_id": 99999, "employers_id": 9999 }' 'http://localhost:8080/api/v1/economics-stats/Sevilla/2009'

POST a un recurso  debe dar un error de método no permitido.
