Investment in R & D, free software in the university and ranking of universities.

Project registered in the course of Service Oriented Systems (Sistemas Orientados a Servicios) (2016/2017) at Univeristy of Sevilla

Project description:
Analysis of the relationship between the level of investment in R & D and education, the spread of free software by universities and position of universities in the ranking of Shanghai.
